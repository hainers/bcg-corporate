BCG Culture

Company Culture and Values

Over more than a decade our CEO Justinas and COO Simonas and their long-standing team have built a collection of market-leading businesses with strong brands. Every day we connect buyers and sellers and facilitate transactions from cars to real estate, job offers to services and consumer goods from professional and private listers. The digital marketplaces we operate promote trust, fairness and efficiency.

Our team

We have fun and enjoy working together and that is our superpower. BCG has a dynamic and motivated team. More than 130 employees and a leadership team of an average tenure at BCG of 12 years makes us specialists in our field. We are part of a fast growing and dynamic company.

Our people

We have an average of 6 years of tenure.

We have a split of 55% male 45% female colleagues.

Our offices

BCG is organised as a matrix structure with central senior management in Lithuania. BCG has offices in Vilnius (LT), Riga (LV), Tallinn and Tartu (EE)
