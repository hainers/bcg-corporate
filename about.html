About

Baltic Classifieds Group (BCG) is a portfolio of online classifieds platforms in the Baltic states (Lithuania, Estonia, Latvia), specializing in four business lines – automotive (Autoplius.lt, auto24.ee), real estate (Aruodas.lt, KV.ee, City24.ee, City24.lv), jobs & services (CVbankas.lt, Paslaugos.lt) and generalist (Skelbiu.lt, Osta.ee, Kainos.lt, Kuldnebors.ee). The company has headquarter in Vilnius (Lithuania) and offices in Tallinn (Estonia), Tartu (Estonia) and Riga (Latvia).

Structure

BCG operates across Baltics through the companies: Diginet LTU in Lithuania, AllePal OÜ and Kinnisvaraportaal OÜ in Estonia, and City24 SIA in Latvia. Lithuania is the largest market for BCG where the company owns Autoplius.lt, Skelbiu.lt, Aruodas.lt, CVbankas.lt, Kainos.lt and Paslaugos.lt. Estonia is it’s second largest, with a uto24.ee, KV.ee, City24.ee, Osta.ee and KuldneBörs.ee. In Latvia, BCG owns City24.lv.

Experienced team

More than 130 employees and a leadership team of an average tenure at BCG of 12 years. BCG is organised as a matrix structure with central senior management in Lithuania. BCG has offices in Vilnius (LT), Riga (LV), Tallinn and Tartu (EE).

Sustainability

In BCG we see users as the environmental superheros. Our classified portals connect buyers and sellers with pre-loved goods and give a second life to those products reducing environmental footprint and making the world a greener place.
