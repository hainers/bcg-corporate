About

Baltic Classifieds Group (BCG) is a portfolio of twelve online classifieds portals in the Baltic states (Lithuania, Estonia, Latvia), comprising of four business lines – (1) automotive (Autoplius.lt (Lithuania) and Auto24.ee (Estonia)), (2) real estate (Aruodas.lt (Lithuania), KV.ee and City24.ee (Estonia), City24.lv (Latvia)), (3) jobs & services (CVbankas.lt and Paslaugos.lt (Lithuania)) and (4) generalist (Skelbiu.lt and Kainos.lt (Lithuania), Osta.ee and Kuldnebörs.ee (Estonia)). BCG has headquarters in Vilnius (Lithuania) and other offices in Tallinn (Estonia), Tartu (Estonia) and Riga (Latvia).

Structure

BCG operates across Baltics through the companies: Diginet LTU in Lithuania, AllePal OÜ and Kinnisvaraportaal OÜ in Estonia, and City24 SIA in Latvia. Lithuania is the largest market for BCG where the company owns Autoplius.lt, Skelbiu.lt, Aruodas.lt, CVbankas.lt, Kainos.lt and Paslaugos.lt. Estonia is it’s second largest, with Auto24.ee, KV.ee, City24.ee, Osta.ee and KuldneBörs.ee. In Latvia BCG owns City24.lv.

Experienced team

As of 31 January 2021, BCG had more than 130 employees and a leadership team with an average tenure at BCG of twelve years. BCG is organised as a matrix structure with central senior management in Lithuania. BCG has offices in Vilnius (Lithuania), Riga (Latvia), Tallinn and Tartu (Estonia).

Sustainability

At BCG we see our users as the environmental superheros. Our classified portals connect buyers and sellers with pre-loved goods and give a second life to products, providing a channel of green commerce to divert such goods from landfill, recycling or disuse, and allow increasingly environmentally conscious consumers and businesses to reduce their environmental impact and help make the world a greener place.
