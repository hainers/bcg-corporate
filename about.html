About

Baltic Classifieds Group (BCG) operates 12 portals in 4 markets. Our portfolio includes automotive, real estate, general and jobs portals ranking no. 1 in all markets. BCG 12 portals are among Top the most visited in respective country.

Structure

The company operates in Lithuania, its largest market, through Diginet LTU, in Estonia through AllePal OÜ, and in Latvia through City24.

Experienced team

133 employees and a leadership team of an average tenure at BCG of 12 years. BCG is organised as a matrix structure with central senior management in Lithuania. BCG has offices in Vilnius (LT), Riga (LV), Tallinn and Tartu (EE)
